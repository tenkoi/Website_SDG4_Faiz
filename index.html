About SDG 4

What is SDG 4?

SDG 4 aims to provide quality education that is inclusive, equitable, and promotes lifelong learning. It addresses issues like access to education, teacher training, and literacy rates.

Why It Matters

Education is a fundamental human right and a key driver for sustainable development. It empowers individuals, reduces inequalities, and fosters innovation.
